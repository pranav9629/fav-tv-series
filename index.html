#Sneak peek on my all time favorite TV shows!

Dragon Ball Z

This show has been my first TV show experience! TV serials are meant to end with a thrilling and nail biting suspense and this is truly one such show which makes you keep thinking "What's next?"

F.R.I.E.N.D.S

As title speaks off, it is all about friendship. A lot of sarcasam to make you die with laughter. An episode of this show can make your day awesome! Re-watching has never been boring with this show.

Game of thrones

While kingdoms fight against each other and are on the race to gain power, white walkers(kind of zombies..) are on their way to demolish everyone and that's quite unexpected. The three dragons flaring smoke all around enemies is truly a treat to eyes and the show deserves worth a watch.

PUNISHER

A thrilling TV series and falls truly under the category of "Netflix and chill" on a pleasent friday evening.

Stranger Things

Story being dated back to 80's, it truly takes our imagination to those olden days. With stangest of things happening all over the town, a bunch of kids who consider themselves as geeks, solves the mystery..and yes! not to forget unimaginable power of Eleven.
